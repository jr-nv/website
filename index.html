Romans 3:23

For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God

Everyone has messed up in their lives. If we are being truly honest, we must admit that we've hurt ourselves and others.The Bible calls this sin. If we die in our sins, we will be eternally separated from God, and lost in hell forever.

Romans 5:8

But God demonstrates His own love toward us, in that while we were still sinners, Christ died for us.

Because God is holy and just, sin must be punished. God cannot just let it slide. The good news is that Jesus stepped in and paid the price for our sin. He took our punishment upon himself. He died for our sins and rose again.

Romans 6:23

For the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord

God is offering you a place in heaven as a free gift. All you have to do is repent of your sins and place your faith in Jesus Christ for the forgiveness of your sins and the salvation of your soul. Receive the gift today!

Ephesians 2:8-9

For by grace you have been saved through faith, and that not of yourselves; it is the gift of God, not of works, lest anyone should boast

You cannot earn your way to heaven with money or good deeds. It is only through the shed blood of Jesus Christ that you can be saved. Don't let pride and arrogance keep you from receiving the gift of eternal life.

Romans 10:13

For “whoever calls on the name of the Lord shall be saved.”

Jesus will save all who call upon His name. In this verse, we are reminded that salvation does not rest on our strength, our consistency, or our worthiness, but on the gracious promise of God Himself. We can be confident that Jesus will save us if we simply call upon Him.

Hebrews 9:27

And as it is appointed for men to die once, but after this the judgment

There are no second chances. This isn't a cheap scare tactic, it is the actual truth. The decision you make in this life matters!
